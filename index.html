Frequently Asked Questions

What are tokens and how are they used?
Tokens are used for AI-powered features like content generation, personalization, and automated editing. Each plan includes a generous quarterly allocation, with the ability to add more as needed. Most teams find the included tokens more than sufficient for their needs.
Can I change plans anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, and we'll prorate any differences.
How does the quarterly allocation work?
Your video hours and tokens are distributed quarterly to ensure consistent usage throughout the year. Single Brand plans receive 5 hours and 250 tokens per quarter, while Multi-Brand plans get slightly more per brand. Unused allocations don't roll over, encouraging regular content creation.
What video formats do you support?
We support all major video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, and more. Our platform can output to MP4, HLS, and other formats optimized for various channels.
Can I switch between Single and Multi-Brand plans?
Yes! You can upgrade from Single to Multi-Brand plans at any time. We'll prorate your payment and ensure a smooth transition. The Multi-Brand plan offers better value per brand and includes additional collaboration features.
